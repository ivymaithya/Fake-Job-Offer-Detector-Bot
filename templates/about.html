Fake Job Offer Detector Bot

A University Software Engineering Project

Purpose

The Fake Job Offer Detector Bot is designed to help students and job seekers identify potentially fraudulent job advertisements, internship opportunities, and recruitment messages. With the rise of employment scams, it can be difficult to distinguish legitimate offers from malicious ones.

How It Works

The system uses a rule-based algorithm to analyze the text of a job advertisement. It scans the text for specific suspicious keywords and phrases that are commonly associated with employment scams.

Based on the detected keywords, the system assigns a risk score from 0 to 100:

  • 0-30: Safe - No obvious signs of fraud.
  • 31-60: Suspicious - Contains some potentially misleading or scam-like phrasing. Proceed with caution.
  • 61-100: Likely Scam - Contains severe red flags, such as requests for upfront payment. Highly likely to be a scam.
